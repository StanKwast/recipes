Tiramisu

Dessert, Italian

Description: A classic Italian dessert with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and creamy mascarpone filling.

Ingredients:

  • Espresso/Coffee
  • Eggs 4
  • Sugar 125 g
  • Mascarpone 500 g
  • Long finger cookies 250 g
  • Cacao powder
  • Coffee Liquor

Instructions:

  1. Brew some espresso and pour it into a deep plate/bowl.
  2. Separate the egg whites from the egg yolks. (We will use the egg whites later.)
  3. Mix the sugar with the egg yolks and stir until a smooth cream forms. Then, add the mascarpone and continue stirring until well combined.
  4. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  5. Gently fold the stiff egg whites into the mascarpone mixture, a bit at a time.
  6. (Optional) Add coffee liquor to the espresso, to taste.
  7. Dip the long finger cookies in the espresso, ensuring they are soaked but not falling apart. Let them drain slightly and use them to cover the bottom of a pie mold.
  8. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the soaked cookies. Repeat the cookie and mascarpone layers.
  9. Place the tiramisu in the fridge for a few hours (overnight is preferable).
  10. Before serving, dust the tiramisu with cocoa powder using a sieve.

Red Lentil Dal

Main Course, Indian, Curry

Description: A comforting, flavor-packed Indian dal perfect for colder days. This rich lentil stew, infused with warming spices and creamy coconut, is a must-try whether you're vegan or not.

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil 15 ml (1 tbsp)
  • Bay leaf 1
  • Cumin seeds 10 g (1 tbsp)
  • Onion (medium) 1
  • Garlic cloves 3
  • Ginger 5 cm piece
  • Chili 1
  • Ground coriander 2.5 g (1/2 tsp)
  • Garam masala 5 g (1 tsp)
  • Turmeric 2.5 g (1/2 tsp)
  • Canned tomatoes 400 g
  • Vegetable broth 480 ml (2 cups)
  • Red lentils 190 g (1 cup)
  • Coconut cream 200-400 ml (1/2 - 1 can)
  • Lime juice 30 ml (from 1/2 lime)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Nut butter (optional) 15 g (1 tbsp)
  • Fresh cilantro a handful

Instructions:

  1. Heat a pan and add olive oil, cumin seeds, and the bay leaf. Sauté for 30-60 seconds.
  2. Add the onion and cook until translucent.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, and chili, cooking for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. Stir in the ground spices (coriander, garam masala, turmeric) and cook for 30-60 seconds, stirring frequently to avoid burning.
  5. Pour in the tomatoes, lentils, and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 20 minutes or until the lentils are fully cooked.
  6. Stir in the coconut cream, lime juice, and nut butter (if using). Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Add fresh cilantro and cook for another minute.
  8. Serve the dal with rice, naan, vegan yogurt, or your preferred sides.

Harissa Chickpea Stew

Main Course

Description: A warming and spicy chickpea stew with rich harissa and smoked paprika, topped with a tangy lemon-garlic yogurt drizzle and served with toasted sourdough. The perfect cozy meal for cold weather.

Ingredients:

  • Chickpeas (tin) 1 can
  • Chopped tomatoes (tin) 1 can
  • Red onion (small) 1
  • Garlic cloves 3
  • Harissa 15 g (3 tsp)
  • Smoked paprika 2.5 g (1 tsp)
  • Stock cube 1
  • Lemon 1/2 (for drizzle)
  • Unsweetened dairy-free yoghurt 45 g (3 tbsp)
  • Garlic (for drizzle) 1 clove
  • Chopped parsley optional
  • Chilli flakes optional
  • Sourdough bread (for toasting) optional

Instructions:

  1. Finely slice the red onion and garlic. Heat a splash of oil in a pot, add the onion and garlic, and soften for 5 minutes over medium heat.
  2. Stir in the harissa and chickpeas, cooking for a couple of minutes to coat the chickpeas.
  3. Add the tin of chopped tomatoes and crumble in the stock cube (or mix in a mug with hot water and pour in).
  4. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then simmer for a further 10 minutes, stirring occasionally to reduce the liquid.
  5. For the drizzle, crush, grate, or finely chop the garlic. Add to a small bowl with the yoghurt, zest and juice of the lemon, and mix. Season to taste.
  6. Serve the stew in a bowl, drizzle with the yogurt mixture, and add any optional toppings like parsley, chili flakes, or toasted sourdough.

Buffalo Chickpea Stuffed Sweet Potato

Main Course, Oven

Description: A delicious and spicy dish combining roasted sweet potatoes with buffalo chickpeas, offering a perfect blend of heat, sweetness, and savory flavors. Top with your favorite fixings for an irresistible meal.

Ingredients:

  • Sweet potato (medium or large) 1
  • Chickpeas (drained) 425 g (1 can)
  • Garlic powder 2.5 g (1/2 tsp)
  • Smoked paprika 2.5 g (1/2 tsp)
  • Cumin 2.5 g (1/2 tsp)
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Hot sauce 60 ml (1/4 cup)
  • Culinary vinegar (e.g., apple cider vinegar) 15 ml (1 tbsp)
  • Tahini or cashew butter 30 g (2 tbsp)
  • Maple syrup 2.5 g (1/2 tsp)
  • Soy sauce or aminos 15 ml (1 tbsp)
  • Ranch (optional) to taste
  • Cilantro (optional) to taste
  • Scallions (optional) to taste
  • Vegan yogurt (optional) to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F). Place the sweet potato halves face down on a sheet pan lined with parchment paper. Roast for 20 minutes.
  2. While the sweet potato is roasting, toss the chickpeas in garlic powder, smoked paprika, cumin, and a light coating of oil. After 20 minutes, add the chickpeas to the sheet pan with the sweet potato and roast for an additional 25 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, mix together all the buffalo sauce ingredients (hot sauce, vinegar, tahini/cashew butter, maple syrup, and soy sauce/aminos).
  4. Once the chickpeas are done roasting, toss them in the buffalo sauce until fully coated.
  5. Let the sweet potatoes cool for 5 minutes, then top them with the buffalo chickpeas. Add any optional toppings like ranch, cilantro, scallions, or vegan yogurt.

Baked Cauliflower Parmesan

Main Course, Oven, Italian

Description: A healthier twist on classic Parmesan, featuring cauliflower coated in a flavorful breading, topped with tangy tomato sauce and melted mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Cauliflower (head) 560 g
  • Almond flour 80 g (2/3 cup)
  • Grated Parmesan 80 g (2/3 cup)
  • Italian seasoning 5 g (1 tbsp)
  • Garlic powder 1.5 g (1/2 tsp)
  • Black pepper 1 g (1/4 tsp)
  • Oregano 1 g (1 tbsp)
  • Paprika 2.5 g (1 tsp)
  • Salt 3.5 g (3/4 tsp)
  • Egg 1
  • Tomato paste 30 g (2 tbsp)
  • Water 120 ml (1/2 cup)
  • Italian seasoning (for sauce) 0.5 g (1/2 tsp)
  • Salt (for sauce) pinch
  • Pepper (for sauce) pinch
  • Shredded mozzarella 120 g (1 cup)
  • Fresh parsley (optional) to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 175°C (350°F).
  2. Cut the cauliflower into 2/3-inch slices and set them aside.
  3. In a flat plate, mix together almond flour, grated Parmesan, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, black pepper, oregano, paprika, and salt. Set aside.
  4. In another flat plate, whisk the egg with a pinch of salt and pepper. Dip the cauliflower slices into the egg on both sides, then transfer to the breading mixture, ensuring both sides are coated. Place the coated cauliflower on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Repeat until all slices are coated.
  5. Bake the cauliflower for 15 minutes. While baking, mix together the sauce ingredients: tomato paste, water, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  6. After 15 minutes, remove the cauliflower from the oven and add the sauce and mozzarella cheese on top. Return to the oven and bake for another 15 minutes.
  7. Top the cauliflower parmesan with fresh parsley, serve, and enjoy!

Rice Paper Dumplings

Appetizer, Snacks

Description: These crispy rice paper dumplings are packed with delicious flavors and are perfect for dipping!

Ingredients:

  • Garlic 4 cloves, minced
  • Carrots 2, grated
  • Tofu 1 block, grated
  • Medium cabbage 1/4, chopped
  • Mushrooms 250 g, diced
  • Parsley 1 bunch, chopped
  • Soy sauce 1/4 cup (use gluten-free if needed)
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Rice paper sheets 20 sheets

Instructions:

  1. Add garlic to a pan with oil, then add tofu and fry for about 3 minutes.
  2. Add the rest of the vegetables and fry for 5 more minutes. Season with soy sauce and continue frying until softened. Remove from heat.
  3. To make the dumplings: Dip each rice paper sheet in cold water for 10 seconds and place it on a wooden board.
  4. Add about 1/4 cup of the mixture to the center of the rice paper. Fold the sides, then the bottom, and finish with the top to seal the dumpling.
  5. Wrap each dumpling with a second layer of rice paper as you did with the first.
  6. Once all dumplings are wrapped, heat oil in a large pan and fry the dumplings on both sides until golden and crispy.
  7. For the dipping sauce, mix all the sauce ingredients together. Serve the dumplings with the sauce and enjoy!

Eggplant Tacos

Main Course, Mexican

Description: A great meat substitute, these eggplant tacos are full of flavor and easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • Eggplants 3
  • Cumin 1 tsp
  • Paprika 1 tsp
  • Salt 1 tsp
  • Garlic powder 1/2 tsp
  • Chili powder 1/2 tsp
  • BBQ sauce 3-4 tbsp

Instructions:

  1. ROAST eggplants at 200°C/400°F for 20 minutes or until the skin starts to look wrinkled.
  2. Place roasted eggplants in a bowl, cover for 5 minutes.
  3. Peel the eggplants and tear into strips.
  4. Mix cumin, paprika, salt, garlic powder, and chili powder. Toss with the eggplant.
  5. BAKE at 200°C/400°F for 20 minutes.
  6. Combine with BBQ sauce and serve as taco filling.

Al Pastor Style Cauliflower

Main Course, Oven

Description: A delicious, spicy cauliflower dish with a tangy pineapple sauce. Perfect as a vegetarian taco filling or side dish.

Ingredients:

  • Cauliflower 1 large
  • Onion 1
  • Garlic cloves 4
  • Red chilis 4
  • Pineapple juice 60 ml (1/4 cup)
  • White wine vinegar 2 tbsp
  • Oil 3 tbsp
  • Cumin 1 tsp
  • Paprika 2 tsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. BOIL cauliflower in salted water for 5-10 minutes.
  2. Remove cauliflower and let it steam fry for 10 minutes.
  3. For the sauce: Toast onion, garlic, and red chilis in a pan for 5-10 minutes until softened.
  4. Blend the sautéed vegetables with pineapple juice, white wine vinegar, oil, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  5. Cover the cauliflower with the sauce mixture.
  6. BAKE at 225°C/450°F for 45 minutes.

Stuffed Bell Peppers with Feta

Main Course, Oven

Description: A delicious vegetarian dish with roasted bell peppers filled with feta cheese and crispy breadcrumbs. Serve with a squeeze of lime for extra flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Bell peppers 3
  • Whole wheat flour 1 cup
  • Panko breadcrumbs 1 cup
  • Eggs 2
  • Feta cheese 1/2 block
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Lime 1/2
  • Olive oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. CUT the bell peppers in half, remove seeds, and bake in the oven at 250°C (482°F) for 15 minutes, until the skin blackens and the peppers are soft.
  2. PEEL the bell peppers and place a small piece of feta cheese in each half. Optionally secure with a toothpick.

  4. DIP the pepper-feta halves into the flour, then the egg, and finally coat in panko breadcrumbs.
  5. FRY in a pan with plenty of olive oil and serve with lime.

Chinese Cucumber Salad

Side Dish, Salad

Description: A fresh, tangy, and flavorful childhood favorite with cucumbers marinated in a savory and slightly sweet sauce, topped with chili oil and sesame seeds.

Ingredients:

  • Cucumbers 2 large or 3 medium
  • Sea salt to taste
  • Black vinegar 1.5 tbsp
  • Soy sauce 1 tbsp
  • Sesame oil 1 tsp
  • Liquid sweetener or sugar 2 tsp
  • Garlic 2 cloves, minced
  • Chili oil to taste
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. WASH the cucumbers, slice off the ends, and slice at an angle using wooden chopsticks.
  2. PLACE cucumbers in a bowl, sprinkle with salt to draw out moisture, set aside for 5 minutes, then rinse and drain. Alternatively, skip this step and marinate the cucumber directly in the sauce.
  3. IN A BOWL, mix black vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, sweetener, garlic, and chili oil.
  4. MIX cucumbers with the sauce until well-coated.
  5. TRANSFER to a plate, spoon sauce on top, and garnish with sesame seeds and extra chili oil.

Roasted Eggplant with Harissa-Tahini Sauce

Main Course

Description: Double-roasting the eggplant creates a custardy texture with caramelized edges, paired with a flavorful harissa-tahini sauce and garnished with roasted cashews and fresh herbs.

Ingredients:

  • Globe eggplants as needed
  • Extra-virgin olive oil for roasting
  • Lower-sodium soy sauce or tamari to taste
  • Mild harissa to taste
  • Maple syrup to taste
  • Kosher salt to taste
  • Chopped roasted cashews for garnish
  • Fresh herbs (parsley or mint) for garnish
  • Tahini for sauce
  • Fresh lemon juice for sauce
  • Fresh garlic for sauce

Instructions:

  1. PREHEAT oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. SLICE eggplants in half lengthwise and score the flesh.
  3. BRUSH with olive oil and season with salt. Roast for 30-40 minutes until golden and caramelized.

  5. REMOVE eggplant from the oven, drizzle with sauce, and garnish with chopped roasted cashews and fresh herbs.
  6. SERVE and enjoy!

Tiger Skin Eggs

Side Dish

Description: A savory and spicy Chinese dish where hard-boiled eggs are fried to form a crispy skin, which soaks up a delicious sauce made with soy sauce, mirin, gochujang, and beer.

Ingredients:

  • Hard-boiled eggs 6-8 eggs
  • Soy sauce 1/2 cup
  • Mirin 1/3 cup
  • Rice vinegar 1/3 cup
  • Gochujang (Korean chili paste) 1 1/2 tbsp
  • Diced Thai chilis 4 chilis
  • Diced garlic cloves 4 cloves
  • Beer 1 full can
  • Sugar 2 tbsp
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. PEEL the hard-boiled eggs and set aside.
  2. HEAT a little oil in a pan and fry the eggs until the skin becomes crispy.
  3. IN a separate pan, combine soy sauce, mirin, rice vinegar, gochujang, diced Thai chilis, garlic, beer, sugar, and salt. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5-7 minutes to blend the flavors.
  4. ADD the fried eggs to the sauce and cook for another 5-10 minutes, occasionally turning the eggs so they absorb the sauce.
  5. SERVE hot with the eggs fully coated in the sauce.

Meaty Mushroom Pie

Main Course, Oven

Description: A rich, savory vegan pie inspired by steak and Guinness pie, with a mix of mushrooms, red wine, and a flaky puff pastry crust.

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil to taste
  • Red onion 1, peeled and chopped
  • Garlic cloves 4, peeled and finely chopped
  • Carrots 2, chopped
  • Celery sticks 2, chopped
  • Mixed mushrooms 500g (17.6oz), roughly sliced
  • Vegan-friendly red wine 250ml (1 cup)
  • Fresh rosemary 3 sprigs, chopped
  • Soy sauce 2 tbsp
  • Vegetable stock 250ml (1 cup)
  • Plain flour 3 tbsp, plus extra for dusting
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Vegan cheddar cheese 100g (3.5oz), grated
  • Vegan-friendly puff pastry 500g (17.6oz) block
  • Plant-based milk for brushing

Instructions:

  1. HEAT olive oil in a pan, then fry red onion, garlic, carrots, and celery for 5 minutes.
  2. ADD mushrooms and cook until they shrink in size and release moisture.
  3. TURN heat up and cook red wine until most of the liquid evaporates.
  4. REDUCE heat to medium, then add rosemary, soy sauce, vegetable stock, and flour. Cook until the mixture thickens into a gravy-like consistency.
  5. SEASON with salt and pepper, remove from heat, and stir in grated vegan cheddar cheese.
  6. ALLOW the filling to cool for 20-30 minutes.
  7. PREHEAT the oven to 200°C fan / 425°F.
  8. ROLL out 1/3 of the puff pastry and line a baking dish. Dust with flour to prevent a soggy bottom.
  9. ADD the cooled filling, then brush the pastry edges with plant-based milk.
  10. ROLL out the remaining pastry and cover the pie. Seal the edges, brush the top with milk.
  11. BAKE for 35-45 minutes, or until golden brown all over.

Baked Eggplant with Cheese and Tomatoes

Main Course, Italian

Description: A deliciously baked eggplant with layers of vegan mozzarella, fresh tomato sauce, and Italian herbs, perfect as a side or standalone dish.

Ingredients:

  • Large eggplant 1
  • Fresh tomatoes 2, medium, sliced
  • Vegan mozzarella cheese 250g, sliced
  • Grated tomatoes 200g
  • Garlic cloves 4, minced
  • Dried Italian herbs 1/2 tsp
  • Fresh basil leaves 5-6, chopped
  • Sugar 1.5 tsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Olive or vegetable oil 1 tbsp

Instructions:

  1. PREHEAT the oven to 180°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. CUT the eggplant lengthwise, leaving the stalk intact, and press gently to open.
  3. SEASON the eggplant with salt and let rest for 20 minutes, then pat dry with paper towels.
  4. HEAT olive oil in a pan, then add pureed tomatoes, garlic, sugar, salt, pepper, Italian herbs, and basil. Simmer until the sauce thickens.
  5. SLICE the tomatoes and vegan mozzarella thinly.
  6. BRUSH each eggplant slice with tomato sauce and layer with tomato and cheese slices.
  7. BAKE in the preheated oven for 40-50 minutes until toasted and soft.
  8. GARNISH with fresh basil or parsley leaves and serve immediately or store for later.

Hungarian Mushroom Soup

Soup

Description: A creamy and savory Hungarian mushroom soup, rich with the flavors of paprika, dill, and sour cream. Perfect for winter!

Ingredients:

  • Salted butter 4 tablespoons
  • White onions 1 ½ cups, chopped
  • Mushrooms (crimini, button, etc.) 1 lb, sliced
  • Dried dill weed 2 teaspoons
  • Dried thyme 1 teaspoon
  • Sweet paprika 1 tablespoon
  • Lemon juice 1 tablespoon
  • Garlic cloves 2, grated
  • Broth 2 ½ cups (vegetable or chicken)
  • Soy sauce 2 tablespoons
  • All-purpose flour 3 tablespoons
  • Half and half or milk 1 cup
  • Sour cream or crème fraiche 1/3 cup
  • Chopped parsley 3 tablespoons

Instructions:

  1. MELT butter in a soup pot. Sauté the onions for a few minutes, then add mushrooms. Cook for 8-10 minutes on medium heat.
  2. ADD dill, thyme, paprika, garlic, and lemon juice. Cook for 2 minutes.
  3. ADD broth and soy sauce. Simmer on low to medium heat for a few minutes.
  4. MIX half and half with flour in a bowl, removing lumps. Stir into the soup and simmer until thickened, do not boil.
  5. STIR in sour cream or crème fraiche.
  6. GARNISH with parsley and serve with warm bread and wine.

Pizza Dough

Main Course, Italian

Description: A versatile and fluffy pizza dough perfect for homemade pizzas with a light, chewy crust.

Ingredients:

  • Flour 500 g
  • Salt 1 teaspoon
  • Margarine 80 g
  • Yeast (cube/pack) 1
  • Sugar Some
  • Warm Milk 250 ml

Instructions:

  1. Mix the flour and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Create a small pit in the middle of the flour (large enough to hold the milk and yeast).
  3. Cut the margarine into small pieces (1-2 cm) and scatter them around the pit, ensuring none fall inside.
  4. Place the yeast into the pit. If using a cube, break it into small pieces.
  5. Cover the yeast with a thin layer of sugar.
  6. Gently pour the warm milk over the yeast. (Important: Ensure the milk is not too hot!)
  7. Cover the bowl and let the dough rest in a warm, draft-free place.
  8. Allow the mixture to sit until the center rises.
  9. Mix everything using a dough hook until it forms a smooth dough.
  10. Knead the dough by hand until elastic.
  11. Let the dough sit and rise again.

Chickpea Pumpkin Curry

Main Course, Curry

Description: A creamy and comforting curry made with pumpkin, chickpeas, and a rich blend of Thai red curry paste, tamari, and almond butter. An easy-to-make dish that can be enjoyed with or without oil.

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil or vegetable broth 15 ml (1 tbsp)
  • Shallot (small dice) 1
  • Grated ginger 10 g (2 tsp)
  • Thai red curry paste 30 g (2 tbsp)
  • Pumpkin puree 425 g (1 can, 15 oz)
  • Vegetable broth 240 ml (1 cup)
  • Oat milk (or unsweetened coconut/nondairy milk) 295 ml (1 ¼ cups)
  • Tamari or soy sauce 15 ml (1 tbsp)
  • Fresh lime juice 15 ml (1 tbsp)
  • Almond, peanut, or cashew butter 30 g (2 tbsp)
  • Chickpeas (drained and rinsed, or cooked) 425 g (1 can, 15 oz)
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped cilantro (for garnish) 3 tbsp (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil or vegetable broth in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat. Cook the shallot for about 3 minutes, or until translucent.
  2. Add the grated ginger and Thai red curry paste. Cook for one more minute, stirring constantly.
  3. Add the pumpkin puree, vegetable broth, oat milk, tamari, and lime juice. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer uncovered for 10 minutes, stirring frequently.
  4. Stir in the almond butter until fully dissolved into the curry. Add the chickpeas and cook for another 5 minutes over medium heat, stirring frequently. Add more broth if needed to reach the desired consistency.
  5. Adjust lime juice and season with salt to taste. Garnish with cilantro (if using).

Whole Roasted Sunset Cauliflower

Main Course, Oven

Description: A beautiful and delicious centerpiece for any dinner party. Roasted cauliflower with chickpeas, harissa tahini sauce, and crunchy garnishes.

Ingredients:

  • Cauliflower 1 head
  • Cooked chickpeas 400g tin
  • Olive oil 7 tbsp (for marinade)
  • Garlic powder 2 tsp
  • Smoked paprika 3 tsp (for marinade)
  • Thyme 1.5 tsp
  • Oregano 1.5 tsp
  • Salt 1/2 tsp (for marinade)
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Olive oil 1 tbsp (for chickpeas)
  • Cumin 1/2 tsp
  • Coriander 1/2 tsp
  • Chili powder pinch
  • Salt to taste (for chickpeas)
  • Tahini 100ml (for sauce)
  • Lemon juice 2 tbsp
  • Harissa paste 2 heaping tsp
  • Smoked paprika 1 tsp (for sauce)
  • Salt pinch (for sauce)
  • Water 50ml (for sauce, more if needed)
  • Red pepper for optional sauce color (blended with sauce)
  • Cooked beetroot for optional sauce color (blended with sauce)
  • Nuts/seeds (e.g., walnuts, almonds, sunflower seeds) toasted, for garnish
  • Fresh mint for garnish

Instructions:

  1. PREHEAT oven to 200°C (fan).
  2. BRING a large saucepan of water to boil. Cut the leaves off the cauliflower and trim the stem so it sits flat.
  3. BOIL cauliflower in salted water for 5 minutes. Then let it steam dry on a kitchen towel for 10 minutes. Pat with another towel if needed.
  4. MIX marinade ingredients and coat the cauliflower well. Cover with foil and roast for 30 minutes.
  5. REMOVE foil and add the pre-spiced and oiled chickpeas. Roast for another 15-20 minutes until cauliflower is browned and chickpeas are crispy.
  6. MIX harissa tahini sauce ingredients, and divide into thirds. Blend one third with red pepper, another third with cooked beetroot for color variation.
  7. TOAST the seeds in a dry pan with a little salt, then set aside.
  8. SERVE the roasted cauliflower with chickpeas, drizzle with sauce, and garnish with toasted seeds and fresh mint. Enjoy!

Chana Masala

Indian, Curry

Description: A hearty and flavorful chickpea curry with rich spices.

Ingredients:

  • Vegetable oil 2 tbsp
  • Onion, finely chopped 1
  • Garlic cloves, minced 3
  • Ginger, grated 1-inch piece
  • Green chili, chopped (optional) 1
  • Cumin seeds 1 tsp
  • Ground coriander 1 tsp
  • Ground turmeric 1 tsp
  • Garam masala 1 tsp
  • Ground cumin 1 tsp
  • Chickpeas (400g, drained and rinsed) 1 can
  • Tomatoes, chopped 2 large or 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • Salt 1 tsp, adjust to taste
  • Ground black pepper ½ tsp
  • Water 200 ml
  • Fresh coriander, chopped (for garnish) as desired
  • Lemon juice 1 tbsp

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and cook for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  2. Add the chopped onion and sauté until soft and golden, about 7 minutes.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, and green chili (if using) and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Stir in ground coriander, turmeric, garam masala, and ground cumin. Cook the spices for another minute.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes (or canned tomatoes) and cook until they break down and form a thick sauce, about 10 minutes.
  6. Add the chickpeas, salt, pepper, and water. Stir everything together and simmer for 15 minutes.
  7. Finish with lemon juice and garnish with chopped fresh coriander.

Thai Red Curry with Tofu

Thai, Curry

Description: A flavorful Thai red curry with tofu and vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • Vegetable oil 2 tbsp
  • Firm tofu (300g, drained and cubed) 1 block
  • Onion, sliced 1
  • Red bell pepper, sliced 1
  • Carrot, julienned 1
  • Zucchini, sliced 1
  • Thai red curry paste 3 tbsp
  • Coconut milk 400 ml (optional, or use tomato base for non-coconut)
  • Vegetable broth 200 ml
  • Soy sauce 1 tbsp
  • Brown sugar 1 tbsp
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish) as desired
  • Lime, cut into wedges (for serving) 1
  • Cooked rice (to serve) as needed

Instructions:

  1. Heat 1 tbsp of vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the tofu cubes and cook until golden brown on all sides.
  2. In the same pan, heat the remaining 1 tbsp of oil. Add the onion, bell pepper, carrot, and zucchini. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add the red curry paste and stir to coat the vegetables. Cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk (or tomato base), vegetable broth, soy sauce, and brown sugar. Stir to combine and bring to a simmer.
  5. Return the tofu to the pan and simmer for another 10 minutes.
  6. Serve with cooked rice, garnished with fresh basil and lime wedges.

Sweet Potato and Spinach Curry

Curry, Indian

Description: A warm and comforting curry with sweet potatoes and spinach.

Ingredients:

  • Vegetable oil 2 tbsp
  • Onion, finely chopped 1
  • Garlic cloves, minced 2
  • Ginger, grated 1-inch piece
  • Sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed 2 large
  • Fresh spinach 200 g
  • Ground cumin 1 tsp
  • Ground coriander 1 tsp
  • Ground turmeric 1 tsp
  • Ground cinnamon ½ tsp
  • Canned diced tomatoes 1 can (400 g)
  • Water or vegetable broth 200 ml
  • Salt to taste
  • Ground black pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish) as desired
  • Lime juice 1 tbsp

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Stir in cumin, coriander, turmeric, and cinnamon. Cook for another minute.
  4. Add the cubed sweet potatoes, tomatoes, and water or vegetable broth. Stir to combine, then cover and simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Add the fresh spinach to the pan and stir until wilted.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and lime juice. Garnish with fresh cilantro.

Chili Sin Carne

Main Course, Mexican

Description: A hearty, flavorful vegan chili packed with vegetables, beans, and spices. Perfect for a cozy meal!

Ingredients:

  • Red onion 1/2, finely chopped
  • Garlic 3 cloves, minced
  • Scallions 1 bunch, finely sliced
  • Carrots 3, finely chopped
  • Celery 3 stalks, finely chopped
  • Cilantro 1 bunch, finely chopped
  • Jalapeno 1, finely chopped with seeds removed
  • Cacao or cocoa powder 1 tbsp (optional, enhances flavor)
  • Nutritional yeast 1 tbsp
  • Chili powder 1 tbsp
  • Maple syrup 2 tsp (optional, enhances flavor)
  • Paprika 2 tsp
  • Cumin 2 tsp
  • Lime Juice from 1
  • Apple cider vinegar 1 tbsp
  • Salt 2 tsp
  • Black pepper 1 tsp
  • Chickpeas 1 can, drained
  • Black beans 1 can, drained
  • White beans 1 can, drained
  • Crushed tomatoes 1 can (28 oz)
  • Vegetable broth 1 cup

Instructions:

  1. Stovetop: Heat a splash of oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, scallions, carrots, jalapeno, and celery. Cook for 8 minutes.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients to the pot. Cover and simmer for 20-25 minutes, until everything is cooked through and veggies are tender.
  3. Instant Pot: Add oil to the Instant Pot. Add onion, garlic, scallions, carrots, jalapeno, and celery. Use the saute function to cook for 8 minutes.
  4. Add the remaining ingredients to the Instant Pot. Cook on high pressure for 12 minutes, and let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes.
  5. Let the chili cool slightly and serve.
